Luxury apartment for sale in the unique Intempo building, Poniente (Costa Blanca - Alicante - Benidorm)

🌟 Elegant Apartment in the Iconic Intempo Tower, Benidorm 🌆

Experience luxury living in one of the most exclusive buildings on the Costa Blanca! Perched high above the 10th floor, this apartment offers breathtaking panoramic views of the Mediterranean Sea and the mountains. The Intempo tower, a symbol of Benidorm's skyline, provides its residents with first-class amenities, ensuring comfort and relaxation at every turn.

Apartment Highlights:

- Two spacious bedrooms with sweeping sea and mountain views.
- Two modern bathrooms, including a luxurious hydromassage bathtub.
- Bright, open living area – the perfect spot to unwind.
- Private terrace to enjoy unforgettable Mediterranean sunrises.
- Fully equipped kitchen with premium appliances: induction hob, oven, dishwasher, and refrigerator.

Intempo Building Features

Living here offers access to world-class facilities.
- Skybar, solarium, and jacuzzi on the spectacular 47th floor with stunning views.
- Outdoor and indoor pools (heated) on the 46th floor.
- Two modern gyms (ground floor and 46th floor).
- Spa and wellness area for complete relaxation.
- Playground and family areas, ideal for children.
- Social club, cafeteria, and coworking spaces for flexible work-from-home options.
- 24/7 security, underground parking, and concierge services for peace of mind.

Why Benidorm? 🏖️

Benidorm is a vibrant Mediterranean gem, famous for its golden sandy beaches, lively entertainment, and year-round sunny climate. Only 40 minutes from Alicante International Airport, the city is perfect for both living and investment. Enjoy a rich lifestyle with nearby attractions like Terra Mítica, Aqualandia, and Mundomar, ensuring fun for all ages.
