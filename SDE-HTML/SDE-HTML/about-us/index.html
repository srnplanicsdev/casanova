Our Story and the charm of the Costa Blanca

MK Premium Properties was born from a deep passion for the Costa Blanca and a desire to provide exceptional real estate services. Founded by Martyna Kamińska, our company is based in Altea, a place that embodies the natural beauty and unparalleled quality of life of this region.

Living on the Costa Blanca is a privilege: its stunning landscapes, Mediterranean climate, and vibrant community make it the perfect location to find your ideal home.

At MK Premium Properties, we don’t just help you find a property; we invite you to be part of a fulfilling and enriching life in this Mediterranean paradise.
