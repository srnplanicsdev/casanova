Our services

Comprehensive and efficient for your peace of mind

At MK Premium Properties, we offer a full range of services designed to streamline every stage of buying, selling, and renting properties on the Costa Blanca. Our goal is to ensure a smooth and satisfying real estate experience for every client.

01Complete Property Handling

MK Premium Properties handles all paperwork and administrative tasks for buying or selling a property, ensuring a smooth and efficient process so clients can enjoy their new home on the Costa Blanca with peace of mind.

02Notary Services

We facilitate all the required documentation for signing before a notary. Understanding that bureaucratic procedures can be complex, we provide detailed advice to ensure clients make the best decisions for their peace of mind and well-being.

03Document Translation Services

For international clients, we ensure that all documentation is available in the required language. This facilitates clear communication and understanding throughout the buying or selling process, providing a comfortable and straightforward experience.

04NIE, Residence Permit, and Bank Account Assistance

For those coming from abroad, we assist with obtaining the Número de Identificación de Extranjero (NIE), residence permits, and opening bank accounts in Spain, ensuring a secure and hassle-free stay on the Costa Blanca.

05Property Promotion

We promote properties on national and international real estate portals to attract buyers quickly. Additionally, we use extra marketing strategies to highlight the property and attract the attention of potential buyers.

06Post-Sale Property Management

After the sale, we conduct follow-ups to formalize any necessary changes in utilities, cadastral ownership, taxes, and other administrative matters, ensuring a seamless transition for new owners.

07Recommendation of a certain lawyer, accountant, and certified translator:

We connect you with trusted professionals to ensure that all legal, fiscal, and translation processes are smooth and secured.

08Obtaining licenses and certificates:

We facilitate contact with experts who efficiently handle the process of obtaining tourist licenses, energy certificates, second occupancy licenses, topographical surveys, and much more.

09Recommendation of a technical architect, construction team, and interior designer:

We connect you with specialists in architecture and urban planning, renovation teams, and renowned interior designers who guarantee exceptional results in every project.

10Management of cleaning, home staging, and vacation rentals:

We work with specialized companies in cleaning, home staging, and vacation rental management to maximize the potential of your property.

11Car rental services, parking, transportation, and moving companies:

We offer access to reliable car rental companies, long-term parking, airport transportation, and moving services to ensure everything is set up from day one.

12List of recommended restaurants and tourist attractions:

We provide you a guide of restaurants and tourist spots highly rated by our clients so you can fully enjoy the area.
